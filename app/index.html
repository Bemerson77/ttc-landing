At Trailside Church, our goal is to…

We are nothing but lost without the sacrifice of Jesus on the cross and His bodily resurrection, defeating death forever. In light of that truth, we seek to love Jesus with everything we are and everything we have. Everything we are flows from this truth: That we worship a risen Savior, the author and creator of our faith, who was and is and is to come. By loving Jesus, we are bound to love his creation.

We love the town of Travelers Rest, and as it grows, so do its needs. We are a church which carries service and community in its DNA- it is not a side project for us, rather it is a way of life. Our eventual goal is to be able to fulfill this along the Swamp Rabbit Trail, by offering a place of rest and rejuvenation for the thousands of people using the trail every week. We do this through purposefully living in relationship.

Simply stated, we do not believe that life is meant to be lived alone. We need each other. We put a priority on not giving up meeting together, both as a worshipping family and in small group settings throughout Travelers Rest and the surrounding areas. By living unified, we can work towards the same goals for the good of the people and community: love and serve the community, live and speak the Gospel, and be unified by our own vulnerabilities and shortcomings so that we can be a people of encouragement to one another, spurring each other on toward making the Gospel known in Travelers Rest, SC.
